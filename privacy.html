Privacy Policy

Last updated April 20, 2026. Phavai is currently a public content site. We collect as little personal information as possible while building a useful recommendation experience.

Information you provide

If you email Phavai, we receive the information you choose to send, such as your name, email address, message, and any source links or corrections you include.

Local scoring preferences

Category pages let you save scoring weights in your browser. This preference is stored locally on your device using localStorage and is not sent to Phavai by the static site.

Analytics

Phavai may add privacy-conscious analytics to understand page performance and content quality. If analytics are added, they should be used to improve the site, not to sell personal profiles.

Shopping links

Phavai may link to Amazon, brand stores, or other retailers for shopper convenience. Retailers may receive standard referral information when you click an outbound link. Link availability does not affect rankings.

Data sharing

Phavai does not sell personal information. We may share information if required by law, to protect the site, or to operate services such as hosting, email, and analytics.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@phavai.com.
