Buying questions

Best Standing Desks FAQ

What makes a standing desk trustworthy?

Stability at standing height, warranty support, motor reliability, realistic weight capacity, and clear return policies matter more than flashy controls.

Should I buy a two-leg or four-leg desk?

Two-leg desks are enough for most home offices. Four-leg desks are better for heavy setups, large monitors, and users who are sensitive to wobble at taller heights.

Do standing desks improve health?

They can help reduce long sitting blocks, but the best ergonomic outcome usually comes from changing position, walking, and setting monitor and keyboard height correctly.
