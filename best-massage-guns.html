Buying questions

Best Massage Guns FAQ

Do massage guns actually help recovery?

They can help with short-term muscle soreness and relaxation, but they are not a cure for injury and should not replace medical advice.

What specs matter most?

Amplitude, stall force, noise, weight, and grip ergonomics matter more than having many attachments.

Should I buy a mini massage gun?

Mini models are great for travel and casual relief. For deep tissue work, larger models usually provide more amplitude and power.

Final recommendation

What most shoppers should do

Most shoppers should start with Theragun Prime Plus for a premium recovery experience. Choose Hypervolt 2 Pro for smoother quiet power, Ekrin B37 for value, and Theragun Mini if portability matters most.
