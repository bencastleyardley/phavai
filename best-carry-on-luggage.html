Buying questions

Best Carry-On Luggage FAQ

Hard shell or soft shell?

Hard shells protect contents and look clean, while soft shells often pack more flexibly and handle exterior pockets better.

What size carry-on is safest?

A standard 21- to 22-inch carry-on is safest for domestic overhead bins. Larger carry-ons can work, but airline enforcement varies.

What matters most in luggage?

Wheels, handle stability, warranty, and interior layout matter more than brand aesthetics over years of travel.

Final recommendation

What most shoppers should do

Most travelers should start with Travelpro Platinum Elite for reliability and practical packing. Choose Briggs & Riley for lifetime-warranty confidence, Away Bigger Carry-On for a polished direct-to-consumer hard shell, and Monos Carry-On Pro if front-pocket organization matters.
