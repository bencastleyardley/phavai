Designed for decision psychology, not browsing fatigue.

Every page is comparison-first, direct, and confidence-driven. The top pick is obvious, but the tradeoffs are not hidden.

Collect the signals

Pull credible expert reviews, video reviews, and public owner discussion into one structured dataset.

Score the consensus

Weight each source by trust tier and category relevance, then calculate BestPick, consensus, and signal strength.

Expose the reasoning

Show source-level summaries, confidence indicators, fit warnings, and affiliate disclosures beside the recommendation.
