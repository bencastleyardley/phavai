Buying questions

Best GPS Watches for Ultrarunning FAQ

How much GPS battery do I need for an ultramarathon?

For 50K to 100K races, many modern watches are enough. For 100 miles, leave margin for slow pacing, cold weather, navigation, and aid-station time.

Do ultrarunners need maps on a watch?

Not always, but maps become valuable on unfamiliar courses, mountain races, and long solo efforts where route mistakes are costly.

Is Garmin always best for ultras?

Garmin has the strongest ecosystem, but COROS often wins on battery-per-dollar and simplicity.

Final recommendation

What most shoppers should do

Most ultrarunners should choose the Garmin Forerunner 965 if they want maps and training depth in a lighter package. Choose COROS Pace 3 for value and battery, or Polar Vantage V3 if recovery metrics matter more than Garmin ecosystem depth.
